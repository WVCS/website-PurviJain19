About My Future Aspirations

I have big dreams, and I’m ready to make them happen!

Double Major Dream

I aspire to double major in Computer Science and Entrepreneurship — because why settle for one when you can dominate two fields? My goal is to push the boundaries of technology while building innovative businesses that leave a lasting impact.

Motivating the Next Generation

I am passionate about inspiring others to pursue their dreams in Computer Science—especially to those who think it's "too hard" or "not for me." I want to show them that anyone can master coding, and maybe even get featured on Shark Tank one day with their own tech startup!

Shark Tank Dream

Speaking of Shark Tank...I plan to present my own tech startup and pitch it to the sharks. Why? Because the world needs change-makers like us—people who innovate, create, and change the world one idea at a time. Plus, who wouldn’t want to be on TV with Mark Cuban?

Inspiring the Change

Ultimately, I want to make an impact that inspires people to be fearless in their pursuits, no matter how daunting they might seem. I want to show them that the future is ours to create, and there’s no limit to what we can achieve.
